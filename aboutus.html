About Us

Welcome to GrammarBuddy, a passion project born from a simple idea: that mastering English should be a joyful adventure, not a dreaded chore. We believe a strong foundation in language is a superpower that opens doors, and it should be accessible to everyone, no matter their age or background.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower you to master English skills in a playful and engaging way. We go beyond traditional quizzes, offering a suite of platforms designed to help you think critically and communicate effectively. We offer tools to improve your grammar, spelling, and logical thinking, all within a fun, interactive environment.

Whether you're a young learner just starting your journey, a student preparing for exams, or a professional looking to polish your skills, GrammarBuddy is here for you. We've crafted challenges for every level, from Pre-KG to PhD, because we know that learning is a lifelong process.

  • TaTiTo Code: The TaTiTo Code is a unique, low-tech innovation created to bridge the communication gap between school and home. It is a handwritten, human-readable alternative to QR codes that allows educators to easily share digital resources like quizzes, videos, and worksheets. This makes it simple for parents and students, even those with limited technology access, to retrieve important class materials.
